Leopard 2a7+

The high tech hunter



Armament
  • Rheinmetall 120mm L/55 gun
  • Coaxel MG3
  • FLW 200 remotely controlled weapon station(options for a .50cal machine gun or 40mm grenade launcher) with extra smoke launchers
  • Modern fire control system with thermal gunner and independent thermal commanders sight provide top of the line targeting solutions and a hunter killer system
Protection
  • Arrowhead armor combined with modern composite on turret and hull provides extreme levels of protection
  • Modular added armore for 360 degree protection from light anti tank weapons
  • Smoke grenade launchers on the turret and FLW 200 weapon station give excellent tactical concealment options
Mobility
  • 1500 horsepower MTU MB 873 engine propels the vehicle up to 68km/h
  • Automatic HSWL 354 transmission provides excellent maneuverability, with driver set torque variability, neutral traverse, and variable steering
  • Power pack can be switched out in 35 minutes if it needs to be replaced
  • Torsion bar suspension provides excellent off road mobility and stability
